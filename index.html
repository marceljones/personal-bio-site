A little bit about Me...


Marcel Jones, a native of the Jersey Shore, was born into a large family. Her father spent over 20 years as an active duty soldier in the U.S. Army, being a member of the 555th Parachute Infantry Battalion, also known as the Triple Nickels, the first all black parachute battalion in the history of the U.S. Army.


As the youngest of 7, Marcel had several older siblings to teach and motivate her over the years and support her own interest.


After completing a few years of college, Marcel met and married Roy Puryear, also a soldier and traveled the world for the next 10 years. During that time, they welcome Stephen Anthony Puryear to the family.


While traveling the world ad raising their only child, Marcel worked in the accounting environment no matter where they landed. Somewhere along their travels, she developed a passion for writing fictional stories.


As life would have, Marcel and Roy Puryear are the proud grandparents to 5 year old Avi'Yana Mahri and two year old Kyng Puryear.

Over the years and through my travels, I have met some very interesting people from all walks of life. I have been exposed to a variety of hobbies and activities as well as creative ways to share family time with my native neighbors or transient military families like my own.

As a result, I became accustomed to engaging in a variety of activities I otherwise would have avoided. Some of which became regular hobbies I became quite good at. They are listed below.
